Project initialisation directory structure:

- 3rd-Party
- Audio
    - Music
    - SFX
- Graphics
    - Animations
    - Textures
    - Sprites
    - Fonts
- Licences
- Plugins
- Prefabs
- Resources
- Testing-Sandbox
- Scenes
    - Levels
    - Other
- Scripts

Don't store files in the root, and try not to make new directories in root if possible,
put new directories in sub directories.
